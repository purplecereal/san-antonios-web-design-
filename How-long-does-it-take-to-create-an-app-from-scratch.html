How long does it take to create an app from scratch?

Generally development can take 3-9 months. This can vary by complexity and if you need a pwa, or a native app developed. Sometimes you will need a native app developed. Other times a pwa is better that can function as a website, android, and ios app.



The process

Let’s get more in depth here. First of all you need to tell your developer your idea. Secondly, once you all agree on a price, he would start designing it , after you approve, the designs, the developer would then start development and keep you updated with images, videos etc.

You will need copy ,images, videos, contact info, etc.. or the developer can source them elsewhere. Your app price will largely depend on the details above and how long it will take to create/source these assets.

Design

App design is usually the shortest process and can take days or even a couple weeks in some cases.

The designer/developer would start to build designs, run them by you, once your full approval is met, the designer would pass the assets to the developer.

Development

Your developer would take a look at the design and start developing different pages, sections, etc, he would develop the front end as well as the backend.

Front end development is what you see, back end development is more about the logic to make the front end function.

Sometimes you will need a front end developer, a designer and a backend developer.

In some cases, you will have someone who does all three, while rare, it does happen.

The more the developer does, obviously the higher his/her rate will be.

This also works the same with a web agency.

If the company has really good developers, they will charge you more, but you will get something better and usually faster.

This entire development process will generally take 3 months to a year depending on the app.

In some cases your app will be done in 6 months, in other cases we might be done in 3.

It all comes down to how long the backend and front end will take to develop.

Know your knowledge

If You look closely at this design, you can see it uses an excellent amount of white space, while still using quite a bit of different colors, without ever really abandoning the color theme, which can be a difficult task to accomplish.

This site also provides a great example of how to change background colors of different sections without going overboard on clashing colors, or going to bright or dark, as some sites seem to just ignore completely.



It may also be worth it to notice the consistent color theme.

Using a consistent color palette is extremely useful in making sure your web design utilizes the best use of colors, and really helps draw the eye to what you want your website's viewers to look at.

When your trying to effectively use a white space theme it’s important to make sure your color palette is consistent and the colors you use are relative to the niche your website is covering.
