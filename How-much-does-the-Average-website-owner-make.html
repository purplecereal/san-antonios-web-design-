Website Income

There is a large difference in how much each website can make.
Most of this depends on several things.

1.Does the web site sale any products or services?
2.Does the web site have affiliate sales?
3.Does the web site generate revenue from ads?


After this, we must ask; does the website owner put in effort into seo, marketing and sales? How much effort does he put into it? Does he have a hundred blog posts or does he have a total of three blog posts?

Some websites easily make 10k or 20k a month from ads just from the sheer amount of traffic on their website.

Website Advertisements

To make money from a website's content. The owner has to monetize the site or build out products to generate income. What normally happens is companies post ads on their websites to generate income from each user who clicks the ads.

The more traffic the website gets the more it makes from the ads. This is why content is king and why some websites have so many blog posts. Depending on the ad company you can get anywhere from 10 to 50 dollars per thousand views. A website that gets a thousand views a month could easily earn 500 a month or more depending on the ad agency the owner goes with. Bigger sites that are getting 100k users a month can generate about 7-10k a month depending on the niche.
