Top five web design trend for businesses in San Francisco, California

We did some market research on local websites in San Francisco, California and realized that many of them were not following the current web design trends,so let's talk about it.

1. Pay attention to your use of white space

One of the best things you can do for your websites design is use plenty of white space. This is actually one of the most important of the top 5 web design trends for businesses in San Francisco, California. The thing about white space is it does not actually have to be white. It can be yellow, green, blue etc. It can also be an image or a background design. White space is basically any space on a website that acts as a background. In other words less is more. You don't want to crowd your website with too many things. It distracts your users and actually lowers conversions.



2. Don't use too many fonts

Your use of fonts is huge when it comes to your web design and user retention. If you use too many different fonts it can easily distract the customer and they might not stay on your website for very long. you don't want to distract the user. You do this too much and they will subconciously see your website as unprofessional and untrustworthy.



3. Make sure your website loads fast

The third most important web design trend for your businesss in San Francisco, California is all about speed. You're website needs to be lightning fast to keep todays users intereseted. Does your website remind you of a sloth? If so, CALL NOW! You are going to lose a huge percantage of your customers if you don't fix this. consumers want answers quickly. If you can't solve their problems fast they will find another company who can. Call us now for a free consultion to solve your page speed issues now.



4. Social proof

You need to have social media links on your website. You don't need to have them a the top, but having them somewhere on your website, preferably the footer will go a long way to helping your customers see your business as professional. Customers like to know other people know about your business. it's a sign that the community trusts your company.



5. glass cards

glass cards or glassmorphism is a modern way of showing your customers that you are hip with current trends. Gone are the days where you can put togheher a brown and white website with no design and be seen as professional. The industry is changed and businesses with modern designs are seen as industry leaders.



6. video banners - Bonus tip!

Video banners are the final web design trend for businesses in San Francisco, California. Video banners are still in. However , if you are to use one it better be a high resolution video or consumers will see your website as less then professional. With phones coming equiped with high res video, it's no longer a suggestion.
