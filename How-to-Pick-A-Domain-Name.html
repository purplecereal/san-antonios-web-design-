How to pick a domain name for your business

This article will cover the ins and outs of picking a domain name for our San Antonio Web Design Customers. We will cover how much a Domain name might cost in San Antonio, where you can find one, how you should pick your domain name, if your domain name should include the the term san

Domain Name costs

When you start looking into getting your ecommerce enterprise website built or whatever kind of web design you might need in San Antonio, you want to make sure you get the right domain name. Depending where you buy your domain name you can expect to pay anywhere from .99 all the way to


100k and over in many cases. So how do you pick one? Well you pick it based on your business needs, We’ll cover that in a second. For now, all you need to know is that generally you can expect to pay under 50.00 for a domain name in most cases. Here’s a fun fact, ours was only about 12.99 when we first got it! Now it is worth much more and will probably sell in the thousands if we ever sell it due to all

the SEO work we have done on our website.

Where can you find a domain name?

There are lots of places you can go to find domain name. We usually like to go with namecheap. However, you can choose basically any company that offers web hosting. You can look at our article “the best web design hosting for San Antonio” here for a list of web hosts we recommend.

How do you pick a domain name?

You should pick a domain name that is unique in most cases, it should be the same as your business name. You want your domain name to be unique so that it is easy to search. You also want to keep it short as a general rule of thumb and you don’t want it to have a lot

of hyphens or numbers either, as that can seem spammy. Additionally, you want it to be memorable and easy to spell.



Can my domain name effect seo?

Kinda, in some cases picking a domain name with your city might help you rank, but google has said that it doesn’t really help. It might help to get clicks if users see a keyword in the url, but generally you should pick your domain name as something that you like, that stands out , and is the same as your business name.
