Typical website revenue

There is a big difference in how different website owners can make on average.
Most of this depends on several things.

1. Does the website sale any products or services?
2. Does the website have affiliate sales?
3. Does the website generate revenue from ads?


After this, we must ask; does the average website owner put in effort into SEO, marketing, and sales? How much effort does he put into it? Does he have a hundred blog posts or does he have a total of three blog posts?

Does he have a nice design or did he put minimal effort into that? Did the owner focus on his website speed, onsite, offsite SEO, social media marketing, gorilla marketing, ads, etc? How many products are for sale? How many services does he sell? How much are these products and services priced at? Depending on the answers to the questions above, the website owner could make a few dollars a month or several hundred thousand a year, and in some cases even more.

Some websites easily make 10k or 20k a month from ads just from the sheer amount of traffic on their website.

SEO to help with overhead costs



So we mentioned optimizing your website can help website owners make money, but let's get more depth into that. Now as mentioned there are several ways to optimize your website to help you make more money as a website owner. How well you optimize your website will largely affect how much money you will make. So let's talk about different website owners and how much they would expect. A website owner with no focus on SEO will be spending tons of money on ads, guest posting, influencer marketing, and other types of internet marketing.

A website owner with tons of focus on SEO, where the website has tons of articles with good titles, good descriptions, lots of keywords, and long-form content will spend much less money on ads and will have much better brand recognition. Therefore, the website owner can then afford to make more money from their site by having their website working for them. The average website owner doesn't really put as much focus on SEO as they should and if they did they would make much more money. SEO is used to bring more users to your website and consequently, will get more users to click your ads, and buy your products or services.

When you focus on SEO you also are focusing not only on traffic, but you get a much more targeted response.
